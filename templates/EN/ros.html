Start ROS 1 Noetic master and services

When the robot's API is stopped, click the button below to start ROS (Robot Operating System) services.
From another computer installed with ROS 1 on the same subnet than Poppy, set variable export ROS_MASTER_URI=http://poppy.local:11311 and then use regular ROS commands or MoveIt to drive your robot.
Poppy will be the ROS master. If you do not want that, start ROS controllers from ssh instead.
For more information about ROS usage, please refer to the ROS section of the Poppy documentation.
